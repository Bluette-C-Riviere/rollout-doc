## Account Management

:::info accounts

Please note, your live system login credentials will not work for the Sandbox and vice versa.  
You will need to register an account at  
https://portal-sandbox.fiskaltrust.at/Account/Register as well as  
https://portal.fiskaltrust.at/Account/Register.

:::
